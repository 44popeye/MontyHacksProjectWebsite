About Us!

We are two 16 year old guys that are very into different types of technology. Combining these two technologies allow us to make the great content that you guys are always looking for. It does not matter what Youtube thinks of our videos, it's what you guys think. If you have any ideas please let us know, and we will implement them into our videos.

Another thing that we do a lot of is planning. We are planning to expand our company from just two people, and expand our range of videos from just vlog videos to vlogs and video games. However, we can not do this alone. That is why we are planning out the future piece by piece, and adjusting the plan as needed.

Our Plan

We briefly spoke about our plan in the "About Us" section, but we will go into deeper detail in this section. First, we are a vlog channel, but we would like to expand into a channel that also does video games, especially racing games. We hope to get a sponsorship with a company called OriginPC, this way we can play the racing games at the highest resolution for the channel. OrginPC sells laptops, with desktop grade CPU's and GPU's inside them. In order to record the games that we play, we would need an Elgato Game Capture. Elgato is known very well by the gaming community for how well their game captures work.


Now this is our plan for the near future. We will aways be trying to inovate and improve our channel, which will always lead to a new plan.
